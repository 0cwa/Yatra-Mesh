What is Columba?

Columba is a free, open-source Android messaging app for Reticulum mesh networks. It enables communication without internet, cell towers, or central servers - using Bluetooth LE, WiFi, or LoRa radio via RNode.

Built with Material Design 3, Columba provides a clean interface for sending LXMF messages, making voice calls via LXST, and sharing location data - all with end-to-end encryption and no accounts required.

Getting Started

  1. Download the latest APK from the releases page or via Obtainium

  2. Install the APK (enable 'Install from unknown sources' in settings)

  3. Launch Columba and complete the onboarding flow

  4. Your identity is generated locally - no signup required

Communication Modes

Text Messaging (LXMF)

LXMF (Lightweight Extensible Message Format) is the primary messaging protocol. Messages are end-to-end encrypted and can be stored at Propagation Nodes for later delivery if the recipient is offline.

Voice Calls (LXST)

LXST (Lightweight Extensible Signal Transport) enables voice calls over mesh networks. Uses low-bandwidth codec2 for LoRa-friendly audio.

Location Sharing

Share your GPS coordinates via LXMF messages. Recipients can view locations on an integrated map.

Contacts Tab

My Contacts

Add contacts by scanning their Columba QR code, tapping the star icon in a conversation, or manually entering their LXMF destination hash.

Network Tab

Shows all Reticulum Announces on your network. Filter by: Peers, Relays (Propagation Nodes), or Audio devices.

Network Interfaces

Bluetooth LE (BLE)

Connect to nearby Android/Linux devices. Enable in Settings > Interfaces > Bluetooth LE. Works without internet.

WiFi (TCP/WLAN)

Connect via TCP to public Reticulum gateways or discover local peers on your WLAN.

LoRa via RNode

Long-range, low-power communication using LoRa radio with RNode firmware. Range depends on antenna and environment.

Managing Identities

Your Columba identity is a public/private keypair generated locally. It serves as your LXMF destination address.

Export/Import Identity

Go to Settings > Identities to export your identity as a QR code or base64 string.

Multiple Identities

Columba supports multiple identities. Create new ones in Settings > Identities > Add Identity.

Location Sharing

In any conversation, tap the location icon to share your current GPS coordinates. Recipients receive coordinates via LXMF and can view them on the integrated map.

Troubleshooting

Can't see peers

Ensure you have at least one interface enabled (BLE, WiFi, or RNode). Check that other peers have sent an Announce.

Messages won't send

Verify you have network connectivity. If recipient is offline, messages are stored at your default Propagation Node.

Voice call fails

Both parties must be online simultaneously. Check your interface supports sufficient bandwidth.
